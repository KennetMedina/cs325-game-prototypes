Organ Retrieval

  • High Concept: Kill the robots and recover the stolen organs.
  • Theme: Organ donor.
  • Mandated Variety: For this prototype I used keyboard input. This is a sidescroller type of game, it is a shooter, and there is loot to pick up.
  • Player Experience Goals: To feel excited to be the hero who is trying to save the day.
  • Gameplay: Use the arrows to move and space bar to shoot. Kill the robots and recover the organs they stole.
  • Prototype Goal: For this game I wanted to become more familiar with tilemaps and object collision.
  • Features: There is a drop and collect feature, you must kill the robots and collect the organs they drop. Feature not yet added: time limit.
  • Setting/Premise: There is a villain who has an army of robots and likes to cause chaos. He is your nemesis.
  • Story: You were hired by a hospital to recover the hearts that were stolen by robots. There are people waiting for organ transplants. You must recover all the organs before time runs.
  • Target Audience: Anyone who likes to be the hero.
  • Play Time: Aproximately 5 minutes.
  • Strategies: Since you start at the top of the map, kill the robots at the top and work your way down.
